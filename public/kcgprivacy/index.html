Privacy Policy

Kovach Consulting Group, LLC

Effective: April 3, 2026

Kovach Consulting Group, LLC ("KCG," "we," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly: When you engage our services, we collect your name, email address, phone number, organizational role and context, payment information (processed securely through Stripe — we do not store card numbers), and information you share during coaching sessions including goals, challenges, and organizational details.

Information collected automatically: When you visit our website or use our client portal, we may collect IP address, browser type, pages visited, and access times. We use this data to maintain and improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver coaching and advisory services, process payments and manage billing, communicate with you about scheduling, session preparation, and engagement updates, improve our services and develop new offerings, and comply with legal obligations.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only with payment processors (Stripe) to process transactions, email service providers to send engagement-related communications, and as required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Coaching session content is confidential. We do not share what you discuss in sessions with anyone, except where disclosure is legally required.

Group Coaching Participants

If you participate in a group cohort, other cohort members will see your name and hear information you choose to share during group sessions. All cohort members are bound by confidentiality agreements. KCG does not share your contact information with other participants.

Portal Users (app.kovachconsultants.com)

If you are a KCG client with a portal account, additional information is collected to deliver coaching services. This section describes what is stored, where it lives, and what you can do with it.

What is stored

  • Account information: name, email, phone number, company, and coaching focus.
  • Authentication: a one-way cryptographic hash of your password (never the password itself). If you enable two-factor authentication, a time-based one-time-password (TOTP) secret. Session tokens are stored temporarily and expire automatically.
  • Coaching content: goals, milestones, progress notes, session notes, direct messages with your coach, course progress and quiz scores, community thread messages, and anything you create with in-portal tools (values worksheets, mind maps, bullet journals, sketches, working notes).
  • Payment records: Stripe processes payments on behalf of KCG. The portal stores a record of each transaction (amount, date, status, invoice reference). Card details are not stored in the portal — Stripe holds those directly.

Where it lives

Portal data is stored on Cloudflare infrastructure (Cloudflare D1 database, Cloudflare KV, Cloudflare Pages, Cloudflare Workers). It is encrypted in transit and at rest. Transactional email is sent via Resend. No portal data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. The portal does not use tracking cookies, behavioral analytics, or advertising networks.

Who can see your data

Your coach (Jess Kovach) has access to the coaching content associated with your account — session notes, goals, progress, messages, and coursework — to support the coaching relationship. In community threads, other participants can see only what you post in the thread itself; privacy settings in your account control what other community members can see about your profile. No one outside KCG has access to your data unless legally required.

Your rights as a portal user

  • Export your data. Goals, journal entries, and worksheets can be exported to PDF from within the portal at any time.
  • Request a full copy of all data associated with your account by emailing privacy@kovachconsultants.com.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data via Account Settings or by emailing privacy@kovachconsultants.com.

Portal data retention

Portal data is retained while your account is active. If your account is closed or your coaching engagement ends, data is kept only as long as needed for legitimate purposes (for example, financial records required by law) or at your request. You can ask for earlier deletion at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your engagement is active and for a reasonable period afterward to fulfill legal, accounting, and reporting obligations. Coaching session notes are retained for two years after your engagement ends, then permanently deleted. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure payment processing through Stripe (PCI-compliant), and access controls limiting who can view client data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but take reasonable steps to protect your data.

Your Rights

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, correction of inaccurate information, deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements), or a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, email support@kovachconsultants.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to maintain site functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function properly.

Third-Party Services

Our services may integrate with third-party platforms including Stripe (payments), Calendly (scheduling), and video conferencing tools. Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for adults in professional settings. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we learn we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our practices or legal requirements change. Updates take effect when posted. We will notify active clients of material changes by email.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, email support@kovachconsultants.com.

This policy is provided as a general guide and does not constitute legal advice. Consult with a qualified attorney to ensure compliance with applicable privacy laws, including state-specific requirements, before relying on this policy.
